    Tech Stack / Изисквания

    Currently in Aristocrat , we are looking for a .Net Integration Developer.

    More on the role down below:

    I. Main Duties and Responsibilities

    • Develop, maintain, and tackle new and existing backend services and platform components within the defined scope and timelines.
    • Engage in the implementation of features and technical improvements across the Fusion platform and related systems.
    • Support integration with third-party APIs and external services, ensuring reliable communication and accurate error handling.
    • Perform application testing and debugging, identifying issues and applying remediation where needed.
    • Collaborate with senior developers and team leads in designing and implementing technical solutions.
    • Integrate user-facing elements developed by front-end developers with server-side logic.
    • Build reusable components and libraries to support future development and improve maintainability.
    • Give to optimizing applications for performance, scalability, and reliability.
    • Implement security and information safeguarding mechanisms according to platform standards.
    • Participate in the develop and implementation of data storage solutions.
    • Support investigation and resolution of production issues and technical support tickets when required.
    • Maintain open communication with the direct manager regarding ongoing work, progress, and potential risks.
    • Provide timely progress updates and seek guidance when needed to ensure efficient task completion.
    • Participate in team meetings, sprint ceremonies, and technical discussions.
    • Assist with additional tasks or technical initiatives as assigned by management to support departmental and interpersonal goals.

    II. Information Links and Relationships

    • In daily work, the Back-End Developer reports to their immediate manager according to the approved interpersonal structure of the company.
    • Works closely with other developers, QA engineers, DevOps engineers, product managers, and other team members inside and outside the company.

    III. Requirements

    • 2+ years of professional experience in backend development with C# and .NET technologies
    • Experience with .NET Framework and/or .NET 6/8
    • Experience developing RESTful APIs and backend services
    • Experience with ASP.NET MVC / Web API
    • Experience with Entity Framework or similar ORM technologies
    • Experience with MS SQL Server, including working with schemas, stored procedures, indexes, and query optimization
    • Understanding of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles
    • Experience with API integrations and external service communication
    • Basic knowledge of unit testing
    • Architecture & Design Knowledge
    • Basic familiarity with Domain-Driven Design (DDD) concepts
    • Basic understanding of CQRS principles
    • Familiarity with common design patterns (e.g., Mediator pattern is a plus)
    • Understanding of secure coding practices and data protection principles

    Additional Skills

    • Basic front-end knowledge: HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, Angular (advantage)
    • Familiarity with Agile development practices (Scrum/Kanban)
    • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
    • Ability to investigate technical issues and propose solutions with guidance from senior developers
    • Good communication and collaboration skills
    • Good spoken and written English

    Travel Expectations

    • None

    Additional Information

    At this time, we are unable to sponsor work visas for this position. Candidates must be authorized to work in the job posting location for this position on a full-time basis without the need for current or future visa sponsorship.
